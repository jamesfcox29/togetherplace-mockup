Our Programs

Four ways we support the community

Each program is free or sliding-scale. Walk-ins welcome. We meet you where you are.

🧠

Behavioral Health

Peer support, case management, and connections to counseling and psychiatric care for adults navigating mental health challenges.

Learn more →
❄️

Sanctuary Warming Center

A safe, warm place to sleep on cold nights from November through April. 40 beds. Open nightly 7 PM – 7 AM. No referral needed.

Learn more →
☕

Community Day Program

A drop-in space for connection, meals, showers, laundry, and support services. Open Mon–Fri, 8 AM – 4 PM. Everyone welcome.

Learn more →
🌱

Recovery Support

Peer recovery coaching, group meetings, and MAT coordination for individuals navigating substance use recovery — on their own terms.

Learn more →
